About Me

Dariush Saberi has been in the translation industry for more than 20 years. He founded the first Iranian online translation platform (www.tarjomeh.com) in 2001. He holds BA and MA in Translation and Interpretation. He received his PhD in Computational Linguistics from City University of Hong Kong in 2020. His research focuses on the educational software development, terminology for different purposes from aiding translators with the terminology tools, to academic words for language learners. He is affiliated to The Halliday Centre for Intelligent Applications of Language Studies, City University of Hong Kong. Currently, he cooperates with the International Linguistics Association as their web manager. He has worked on an English-Chinese technical terminology application as a post-doctoral fellow and senior localization manager at Chilin(HK) Ltd, a spin-off of the City University Enterprises Ltd of Hong Kong.
During his PhD studies, he developed a Nominalization and Lexical Formality Application (www.formalwriter.com) in The Halliday Center. It represents an endeavor to design an assisted academic English writing system that automates the nominalization and lexical substitution in ESL/EFL writings based on the most recent Transformer technologies.
He is currently working as Senior AI Developer and Research Lead at i-Learner Education Center
