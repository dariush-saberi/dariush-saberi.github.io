About Me

For over two decades, Dariush Saberi has been deeply immersed in the translation industry. In 2001, he set a milestone by founding Tarjomeh.com, Iran's first online software localization platform, serving more than 400 international companies. Holding both a BA and MA in Translation and Interpretation, Dariush didn't stop there. He earned his PhD in Computational Linguistics from City University of Hong Kong in 2020.

During 2020-2021, his PhD journey led to the creation of the Nominalization and Lexical Formality Application at The Halliday Center, accessible at Github. Utilizing pioneering Large Language Models (LLM) like GPT-2, BERT, and XLM, this application automates nominalization and lexical substitution in ESL/EFL writings, serving as a robust tool for enhancing academic English writing.

Now, in 2023, Dariush is wearing two significant hats. He’s the AI Lead at i-Learner Education Center, where he channels his expertise to drive AI-centric educational initiatives. Additionally, he holds the position of AI Senior Developer for Large Language Models, continuing to push the envelope in the realm of LLM technology and its applications in language and translation. His affiliation with The Halliday Centre for Intelligent Applications of Language Studies at City University of Hong Kong remains a testament to his enduring commitment to advancing the intelligent applications of language studies.

Moreover, Dariush is also making a mark in the international linguistics community as the Web Manager for the International Linguistics Association, further showcasing his multifaceted skills and dedication to the field of linguistics and language technology.
